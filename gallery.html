Superhero Gallery

  • Captain America

    Steve Rogers, also known as Captain America, is a super-soldier who fought in World War II. He is known for his courage, leadership, and the iconic shield he wields.

  • Ant-Man

    Scott Lang, the current Ant-Man, has the ability to shrink in size while increasing in strength. He uses his powers to fight crime and protect the world.

  • Spider-Man

    Peter Parker, a teenager with spider-like abilities, uses his powers to protect New York City from various threats. He is known for his agility, wall-crawling, and spider-sense.

  • Hulk

    Dr. Bruce Banner transforms into the Hulk when he experiences intense anger or stress. The Hulk is known for his incredible strength and durability.

  • Black Widow

    Natasha Romanoff, or Black Widow, is a highly trained spy and former assassin. She is skilled in hand-to-hand combat and espionage, and is a key member of the Avengers.
